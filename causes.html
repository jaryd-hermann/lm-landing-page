Our commitment

Every subscription
does something good.

5% of every active Little Moments subscription goes directly to a cause you believe in. Chosen by you at signup. Donated every month. No exceptions.

5%

Of every subscription. Every month. To a cause you chose when you signed up. Not a one-time thing — recurring, as long as you're a subscriber.

How it works

Simple, transparent, automatic.

No extra steps, no opt-in required. The donation happens in the background every billing cycle.

1

You pick a cause

During onboarding, you choose one of our cause categories — environment, education, mental health, global health, animals, or research.

2

You subscribe

When your subscription payment processes — monthly or annually — 5% of that revenue is allocated to your chosen cause automatically.

3

We donate, every month

Donations are pooled and sent monthly to verified nonprofits in each category through Every.org, our donation infrastructure partner.

Questions

Everything you might want to know.

Is this actually real?

Yes. 5% of every active subscription payment is donated, not just in the first month. This isn't a launch promotion — it's a permanent part of how Little Moments operates, built into the product economics from day one.

What counts as "active subscription revenue"?

All paid subscriptions — monthly and annual. Lifetime memberships receive a one-time 5% donation at purchase. Trial accounts are not included (no revenue, no donation).

Can I change my cause?

Yes. You can update your chosen cause anytime in the app's Settings → Your Cause. The change applies from the next billing cycle.

Which specific nonprofits receive the money?

Donations are distributed to verified nonprofits in each cause category through Every.org. We publish our cause partners and update them annually. You can browse verified nonprofits in each category at every.org.

Can I get a tax receipt?

Donations are made by How They Grow LLC on behalf of subscribers collectively, so individual tax receipts are not available to subscribers. If you need a donation receipt for tax purposes, we recommend making a direct donation through every.org.

What if a cause category is discontinued?

If we need to modify cause categories, we'll notify all affected subscribers at least 30 days in advance and allow them to select a new cause before their next billing date. The 5% commitment does not change.

Is How They Grow LLC a nonprofit?

No. How They Grow LLC is a for-profit company. The 5% pledge is a commitment we make as a business because we believe that if a product captures meaningful moments in people's lives, it should put some of what it earns back into the world. It's not a tax strategy — it's just the right thing to do.
