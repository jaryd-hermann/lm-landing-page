Questions, answered

Is it really under a minute a day?+

Yes. Pick a photo, add a line by voice or text, done. Most moments take about 30 seconds — and Magic Fill lets you catch up several days at once.

Who can see my moments?+

No one but you. Little Moments is a private Capsule, not a social feed. There are no likes, no followers, and nothing is public by default.

What if I miss a few days?+

Nothing breaks and nothing nags you. Magic Fill surfaces the days you have photos but no moment, so you can gently backfill whenever you like.

What does it cost?+

Little Moments is free to download and start capturing. For long term usage like infinite Capsule history and unlimited Threads and Chapters, you can upgrade to an optional premium membership.
